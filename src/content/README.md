# Content Workspace

Store reviewed, structured learning content here by topic cluster.

Each publishable page should maintain:

- page title and canonical route;
- search intent and audience;
- section outline and final English copy;
- terminology and Hanzi review;
- source ledger;
- claim classification;
- editorial and domain review status;
- product, Etsy, Learn, and Bazodiac target links;
- publication and review dates.

Recommended topic directories:

- `wu-xing/`
- `bazi/`
- `zi-wei-dou-shu/`
- `bazodiac-fusion/`

Do not store personal chart data, customer data, credentials, or unpublished sensitive business information.
