12 · Reference

BaZi glossary

Chinese terms follow the CN_SIMPLIFIED region policy. Pinyin is shown with tone marks.

BaZi 八字 bāzì
The eight visible characters formed by four stem-branch pairs.
Four Pillars 四柱 sìzhù
The Year, Month, Day and Hour pillars.
Gan-Zhi 干支 gānzhī
The combined Heavenly-Stem and Earthly-Branch system.
Heavenly Stems 天干 tiāngān
Ten cyclical stem signs classified by Yin/Yang and phase.
Earthly Branches 地支 dìzhī
Twelve cyclical branch signs used for years, months and traditional hours.
Year Pillar 年柱 niánzhù
The year’s stem-branch pair under the selected boundary rule.
Month Pillar 月柱 yuèzhù
The month or seasonal pair under the selected solar-term method.
Day Pillar 日柱 rìzhù
The day’s stem-branch pair.
Hour Pillar 时柱 shízhù
The pair assigned to a traditional two-hour period.
Day Master 日主 rìzhǔ
The Heavenly Stem of the Day Pillar.
Hidden Stems 藏干 cánggān
Stem associations conventionally contained within Earthly Branches.
Five Phases 五行 wǔxíng
Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal and Water as relational phases.
Solar terms 节气 jiéqì
Seasonal markers based on the Sun’s apparent annual path.
Beginning of Spring 立春 lìchūn
A solar term used as a year boundary in many fate-calculation traditions.
Da Yun 大运 dà yùn
Successive approximately ten-year phase cycles in traditional BaZi practice.
HeHun 合婚 héhūn
Traditional chart comparison or marriage-matching practice.
Ten Gods 十神 shíshén
Relational categories derived from the Day Master and other stems.
Useful God 用神 yòngshén
A school-dependent concept for a function considered useful to chart structure.

13 · Evidence

Sources and method boundaries

This guide separates three kinds of support: official calendrical or astronomical sources for technical foundations; accessibility and web standards for implementation; and traditional or scholarly sources for documenting how BaZi is taught. A source can verify that a rule exists in a lineage without proving that the rule predicts life outcomes.

  1. Primary · calendar
    Hong Kong Observatory — Heavenly Stems and Earthly Branches

    Official overview of the ten stems, twelve branches, sixty Gan-Zhi combinations and traditional hour mapping.

  2. Primary · astronomy
    Hong Kong Observatory — The 24 Solar Terms

    Explains the 24 terms through equal segments of the Sun’s apparent longitude.

  3. Primary · convention
    Hong Kong Observatory — What year is it today?

    Useful for distinguishing official calendar year conventions from Lìchūn-based fortune-calculation practice.

  4. Primary · astronomy
    United States Naval Observatory — The Equation of Time

    Defines apparent and mean solar time and explains why longitude and the equation of time matter.

  5. Standard · accessibility
    W3C — Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.2

    Basis for keyboard access, focus visibility, headings, labels and responsive content requirements.
